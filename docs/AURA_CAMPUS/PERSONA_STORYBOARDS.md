# 🎭 Aura Campus — Persona Storyboards

> **Reusable narrative asset** for demos, sales decks, website copy, and marketing.
> Each storyboard follows **Pain Point → Aura Capability → Business Outcome**, grounded in
> the seeded **Aura Demo College** tenant (see [demo_credentials.md](demo_credentials.md) and
> the [DEMO_PLAYBOOK.md](DEMO_PLAYBOOK.md)).
>
> **Scope note:** these describe **currently shipping** capabilities. AI features (Knowledge Hub
> AI summaries / RAG assistant) are marked *(optional / roadmap)* and are not required for any
> storyboard to land. **Last updated:** 2026-06-24.

---

## 1. Chairman — *Rajagopal Menon*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Pain Point** | No single, real-time view of institutional health. Board reviews run on stale spreadsheets compiled by hand across departments, finance, and compliance — by the time numbers reach the Chairman, they're weeks old and impossible to drill into. |
| **Aura Capability** | Institution-wide **KPI dashboards** from one source of truth — students, fee collection, placements, attendance, and accreditation standing, live and drill-down-able. |
| **Business Outcome** | Board-ready visibility in seconds, not weeks. Evidence-based decisions, and confidence in NAAC/NIRF standing without a reporting fire-drill. |
| **Demo proof point** | 6 departments · 3,240 students (UG + PG) · **88% fee collection** · **93% placements** — all live on the dashboard. |

## 2. Principal — *Dr. Lakshmi Narayan*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Pain Point** | Operational firefighting: timetable clashes, faculty-workload disputes, and an accreditation scramble that consumes weeks every cycle — with no early warning when a metric drifts. |
| **Aura Capability** | The **AI Timetable Engine** generates conflict-free schedules in seconds; **faculty workload** is visible at a glance; **IQAC/NAAC readiness**, attendance, fees, and placements are continuously tracked. |
| **Business Outcome** | Weeks of manual scheduling collapse to minutes; management becomes proactive instead of reactive; the institution is *always* accreditation-ready rather than scrambling each cycle. |
| **Demo proof point** | Live timetable solve in **~10 seconds** (OR-Tools engine, in production); IQAC meetings + NAAC evidence already populated. |

## 3. HOD (Computer Science) — *Dr. Suresh Babu*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Pain Point** | Manual departmental coordination — building timetables by hand, tracking CO/PO attainment in spreadsheets, balancing faculty loads, and consolidating CIA results across formats. |
| **Aura Capability** | **Department analytics**, **CIA results + CO/PO attainment** computed automatically, **faculty workload** balancing, an **AI-generated department timetable**, and attendance trends. |
| **Business Outcome** | The department runs on data, not spreadsheets; CO/PO attainment is accreditation-ready on demand; faculty loads are balanced fairly; hours are saved every term. |
| **Demo proof point** | CS department: published CIA marks & results, CO/PO mapping, and a generated timetable draft ready to publish. |

## 4. Faculty — *Dr. Meena Iyer*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Pain Point** | Repetitive admin steals teaching time — paper attendance registers, marks entry across inconsistent formats, hunting for shared resources, and no single place to see one's own schedule. |
| **Aura Capability** | A **Staff Portal** unifying timetable, attendance marking, CIA marks entry, leave, and **Knowledge Hub** upload/access. |
| **Business Outcome** | Less paperwork, more teaching. Attendance and marks flow in one place; teaching resources are a click away. |
| **Demo proof point** | Faculty logs in → sees their timetable, marks a class, enters CIA marks, opens a Knowledge Hub resource — end to end. |

## 5. Student — *Arjun Nair*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Pain Point** | Information is fragmented — attendance %, results, fee dues, and timetable are scattered or require a trip to the office, creating anxiety about eligibility and outstanding dues. |
| **Aura Capability** | A **Student Portal** with attendance, results, **fee dues + online payment (Razorpay)**, timetable, notices, scholarships, and placements — all self-service. |
| **Business Outcome** | Full transparency on standing; no queues at the office; fees paid online in seconds; the student always knows where they stand. |
| **Demo proof point** | Student logs in → checks attendance %, views published results, sees fee dues, initiates an online payment. |

## How to reuse these

- **Live demo:** open with the persona's Pain Point, show the Aura Capability on screen, close on the Business Outcome (see [DEMO_PLAYBOOK.md](DEMO_PLAYBOOK.md) for click-paths).
- **Sales deck (9E):** one slide per persona — Pain → Capability → Outcome — with the demo proof point as the supporting screenshot.
- **Website / marketing:** the Pain→Outcome arc maps directly to landing "Roles" sections and case-study copy.
- **Keep honest:** proof points must match the **seeded demo numbers** (dashboards show real row counts). Update this file if the seed changes.
