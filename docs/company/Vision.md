## Implementation

### Vision statement (verified, from the ecosystem doc)
> *Aura empowers organizations to see, manage, and optimize their operations in real time — across campuses, project sites, and field teams.*

### The three pillars
| Pillar | Delivered through |
|---|---|
| **See** | CCTV · GPS · dashboards · live monitoring (Aura Vision + Aura Insights) |
| **Manage** | Workflows · operations · resources · teams (Aura Flow + products) |
| **Optimize** | Analytics · AI · forecasting · automation (Aura AI + Aura Insights) |

### The strategic bet
Aura is **not** a collection of standalone apps. It is an integrated ecosystem of enterprise applications, all consuming one shared foundation — **Aura Platform** (Identity, Connect, Audit, Docs, Flow, Insights, AI, Mobile, Vision). Build reusable platform capabilities once; deploy them across many verticals (Campus → Build → Field), with **Aura Vision** as the compounding, hard-to-replicate moat.

### Where we are
Aura Campus (education ERP) is the active product and the proving ground; Build, Field, and Vision are planned. Sequencing is deliberate: prove and fund with Campus before expanding.

## Future Roadmap
