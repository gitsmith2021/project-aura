# Company — Mission

## Purpose

State what Aura does today and for whom — the concrete work that advances the [Vision](Vision.md).

## Current State

Aura's active mission is delivered through **Aura Campus**: a modern, multi-tenant Academic ERP for educational institutions.

## Implementation

### Mission statement
**Give educational institutions one platform to run every academic and operational workflow — and stay accreditation-ready year-round — without the cost, lock-in, and multi-system sprawl of legacy ERPs.**

Grounded in what the product actually does (verified in `src/app/layout.tsx` metadata and the shipped modules):

- **One platform** for timetables, admissions, attendance, assessments (CIA), exams, results, finance/fees, payroll, library, hostel, transport, placements, research, scholarships, and the Knowledge Hub.
- **Every stakeholder** served by a dedicated experience: admin, principal/IQAC, HOD, faculty (staff portal), student, parent, and alumni portals.
- **Accreditation built-in:** evidence-ready, structured data for NAAC / NIRF / NBA / AISHE through everyday workflows (CO/PO mapping, CIA ledger, IQAC/AQAR, SSR builder).
- **AI where it helps:** Knowledge Hub summaries and an admin RAG assistant; an OR-Tools engine that builds conflict-free timetables.

### Who we serve
Colleges, universities, autonomous institutions, polytechnics, and teacher-education institutions (Indian higher education first). Reference client: Bishop Heber College (per ecosystem docs).

### How we work
Build each Campus capability so its **generic parts can graduate into Aura Platform** services reused by future products — see [AURA_CORE_ARCHITECTURE.md](../AURA_CORE/AURA_CORE_ARCHITECTURE.md).

## Future Roadmap

- Extract shared services; extend the mission to Build (project execution) and Field (service management).
