# AURA — Company & Ecosystem

**Purpose.** The company-level north star: what the Aura ecosystem is, why it exists, and how its products fit together.

**Scope.** Ecosystem vision, competitive advantage, 5-year strategy, the product portfolio, brand guidelines, and the master glossary. This is *not* a build roadmap — execution lives in each product folder.

**Ownership.** Founder / CEO + product leadership.

**Relationship to other areas.** Sits above everything: [AURA_CORE](../AURA_CORE/) realises the shared-platform parts of this vision; the product folders ([AURA_CAMPUS](../AURA_CAMPUS/), [AURA_BUILD](../AURA_BUILD/), [AURA_FIELD](../AURA_FIELD/), [AURA_VISION](../AURA_VISION/)) execute the portfolio; [AURA_EXECUTIVE](../AURA_EXECUTIVE/) turns it into go-to-market and financial plans.
